Infrastructure & hosting

Hosted on AWS. Secured the way we'd secure our own data.

We live what we preach. Kabrios runs on AWS with the same security controls, operational practices, and monitoring rigor we recommend to our customers.

AWS-native infrastructure

Kabrios is hosted entirely on Amazon Web Services, leveraging AWS's security-first infrastructure including VPCs, security groups, and network isolation by design.

Encryption everywhere

All data is encrypted at rest using AES-256 (AWS KMS-managed keys) and in transit using TLS 1.2+. No exceptions, no unencrypted pathways, no shortcuts.

Tenant isolation

Customer data is logically separated at every layer — database, application, storage, and API. One customer's data is never accessible to another, period.

Identity & access management

Role-based access control with least-privilege enforcement. Administrative access is separated, MFA-protected, and audited. No shared credentials, no standing privileges.

Continuous monitoring

AWS CloudTrail, GuardDuty, Security Hub, and Config provide continuous visibility into security events, configuration drift, and threat detections — the same tools Kabrios integrates for our customers.

Backup & disaster recovery

Automated daily backups with point-in-time recovery. Disaster recovery procedures are documented, tested, and aligned to our business continuity requirements.

Security practices

How we build, ship, and operate — securely.

Secure development lifecycle

Security is embedded in every phase of development — threat modeling during design, static analysis in CI, dependency scanning, code review requirements, and pre-deployment security checks.

Vulnerability management

Continuous scanning of infrastructure and application layers. Critical vulnerabilities are triaged and remediated within defined SLAs. We track every finding to closure.

Penetration testing

Regular third-party penetration testing by qualified assessors. Findings are remediated and retested. Results are available to customers under NDA upon request.

Incident response

Documented incident response procedures with defined roles, escalation paths, communication templates, and post-incident review processes. We practice what we help our customers build.

Change management

All production changes go through a controlled process — peer review, staging validation, staged rollout, and rollback procedures. No cowboy deployments.

Employee security

Background checks for all team members. Security awareness training on hire and annually. Endpoint protection and MDM on all company devices. Least-privilege access to production.

Data handling

Your data is yours. Here's how we protect it.

Data residency

Customer data is stored in US-based AWS regions by default. Data residency requirements for regulated industries or specific jurisdictions can be accommodated.

Data retention & deletion

Customer data is retained for the duration of the service relationship plus a defined wind-down period. Upon termination, data is securely deleted within documented timeframes.

Audit logging

All user actions, administrative operations, and system events are logged with immutable audit trails. Who did what, when, and from where — always attributable, always reviewable.

Access transparency

We log and monitor all internal access to customer environments. Support access requires justification, is time-limited, and is auditable. No standing access to customer data.

Third-party risk

We assess and monitor the security posture of our own vendors and subprocessors. Our approved subprocessor list is available upon request.

Privacy

Kabrios processes personal data only as necessary to deliver the service. We do not sell customer data. Our privacy practices align with applicable data protection requirements.
