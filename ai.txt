Kabrios Trust Center — security, hosting, compliance posture, and operational practices.

Kabrios is built by security practitioners with 25+ years of mission-critical cybersecurity experience across multinational banks, pharmaceutical companies, and US federal agencies including IRS, FBI, Department of Energy, and Health and Human Services.

Kabrios is hosted on AWS with encryption at rest (AES-256) and in transit (TLS 1.2+), tenant isolation, RBAC with least privilege, continuous monitoring via CloudTrail/GuardDuty/Security Hub/Config, and automated backups.

Kabrios is aligned to and actively pursuing certification under: SOC 2 Type II, ISO 27001, NIST 800-53 (full control catalog), HIPAA, and FedRAMP. The certification process has been started for all frameworks.

Kabrios supports certification readiness for customers but does not perform audits or issue certifications.
Certification Coordination is available as an add-on service.
