Kabrios, Inc. ("Kabrios," "we," "us," or "our") respects your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and protect personal information when you visit our websites, use our platform, communicate with us, or otherwise interact with us.

This Privacy Policy does not override any data processing addendum, customer agreement, or other written agreement between Kabrios and a customer that specifically governs the processing of customer data.

1. Scope

This Privacy Policy applies to Kabrios websites and marketing pages, product sign-up and account administration flows, support, sales, security, and other communications with Kabrios, and personal information Kabrios processes in connection with providing its products and services.

2. Personal Information We Collect

Information you provide directly

  • Name, business email address, company name, title, and phone number
  • Account credentials and profile details
  • Billing contact information
  • Support requests, messages, and attachments
  • Information provided through forms, demos, surveys, and sales conversations

Information we collect automatically

  • IP address, browser type, device information, and operating system
  • Usage and interaction data
  • Log data
  • Approximate geolocation derived from IP address
  • Referral URLs, page visit information, cookie data, and similar technology data

Information we receive from other sources

  • Information from your employer or organization when they provision your account
  • Information from third-party integrations you authorize
  • Lead, enrichment, or CRM data from service providers or business partners
  • Publicly available business contact information

3. How We Use Personal Information

  • Provide, operate, maintain, secure, and improve Kabrios
  • Authenticate users and administer accounts
  • Process transactions and manage subscriptions
  • Provide support and respond to requests
  • Send operational notices and service communications
  • Analyze usage trends and improve product performance
  • Investigate security incidents, misuse, fraud, or violations of our terms
  • Comply with legal obligations
  • Market and promote Kabrios, subject to applicable law and your communication preferences

Kabrios does not use customer content to train general-purpose or shared AI models unless Kabrios expressly agrees otherwise in writing.

4. Customer Data Context

When Kabrios processes personal information as part of customer data on behalf of a business customer, Kabrios generally acts as a service provider or processor on that customer’s behalf. In those cases, the customer controls the purposes of processing and is generally responsible for responding to requests relating to that customer data.

5. Cookies and Similar Technologies

Kabrios may use cookies, pixels, local storage, analytics tools, marketing technologies, and similar technologies to keep users signed in, remember preferences, understand site and product usage, measure campaign effectiveness, support marketing and advertising activities, and improve security and performance.

Where required by law, Kabrios will provide appropriate consent choices for non-essential cookies and similar technologies.

6. Legal Bases for Processing

Where applicable law requires a legal basis for processing, Kabrios may rely on performance of a contract, legitimate interests, compliance with legal obligations, consent where required, and other lawful bases permitted by applicable law.

7. How We Share Personal Information

  • With service providers and subprocessors that help us operate the business and Services
  • With cloud hosting, analytics, CRM, support, email, billing, and security vendors
  • With integrations or third-party services that you or your organization enable
  • With professional advisors such as lawyers, auditors, and insurers
  • With regulators, law enforcement, or other third parties when required by law or necessary to protect rights and safety
  • With counterparties in connection with a merger, acquisition, financing, reorganization, or sale of assets

Kabrios does not sell customer data in the ordinary meaning of that term.

8. Sensitive Data Restrictions

Unless Kabrios expressly agrees otherwise in writing, Kabrios is not intended for the processing or storage of protected health information subject to HIPAA, payment card data, Social Security numbers or similar government-issued identification numbers, Controlled Unclassified Information (CUI), export-controlled data, or other similarly regulated sensitive data.

9. Data Retention

Kabrios retains personal information for as long as reasonably necessary to provide the Services, maintain accounts and business records, comply with legal and regulatory obligations, resolve disputes, and enforce agreements. Retention periods may vary based on the nature of the information and the context in which it was collected.

10. Security

Kabrios uses commercially reasonable administrative, technical, and organizational safeguards designed to protect personal information. No security measure is perfect, and Kabrios cannot guarantee absolute security.

11. International Data Transfers

Kabrios may process personal information in the United States and other countries where Kabrios or its service providers operate. Where required, Kabrios will use appropriate safeguards for cross-border transfers.

12. Your Rights and Choices

Depending on your location and applicable law, you may have rights to access, correct, delete, restrict, object to, or port certain personal information, and to withdraw consent where processing is based on consent. You may opt out of marketing emails by using the unsubscribe link in those messages.

To make a privacy request, contact privacy@kabrios.com. If Kabrios processes your personal information on behalf of a customer, you should direct your request to that customer first.

13. California Privacy Rights

If you are a California resident, you may have rights under California privacy law, subject to applicable exceptions, including rights to know, access, correct, delete, and limit certain uses of personal information. You may also have the right not to be discriminated against for exercising applicable privacy rights.

Kabrios does not sell customer data in the ordinary meaning of that term. If California law treats certain analytics, advertising, or similar disclosures as a "sale" or "sharing," Kabrios will honor applicable opt-out rights as required by law.

14. Children’s Privacy

Kabrios is not directed to children, and we do not knowingly collect personal information from children in violation of applicable law.

15. Third-Party Websites and Services

Kabrios websites and Services may link to or interoperate with third-party websites and services. Those third parties have their own privacy practices, and Kabrios is not responsible for them.

16. Changes to this Privacy Policy

Kabrios may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. If we make material changes, we will post the updated version and revise the effective date. Where required by law, we will provide additional notice or obtain consent.
